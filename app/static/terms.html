2. Description of Service

RightG is a geographic address coverage tool that allows users to:

  • Create projects to organize geographic coverage areas
  • Upload polygon boundaries in GeoJSON or KML format
  • Check whether a given street address falls within a defined polygon area
  • Review a history of past address checks

The Service relies on third-party geocoding providers (including OpenStreetMap Nominatim and optionally Mapbox) to convert street addresses into geographic coordinates. We do not control these providers and cannot guarantee their availability or accuracy.

Address coverage results provided by the Service are estimates based on geocoding data from third-party providers and the polygon boundaries you supply. The following limitations apply:
