1. Overview

💡 Short version: We collect your email and name to run your account, store the polygons and address checks you create, and send your addresses to a geocoding service to convert them to coordinates. We don't sell your data and we don't show you ads.

This Privacy Policy explains how RightG ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, and shares information about you when you use the RightG address coverage service (the "Service"). It applies to all users regardless of location.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Providing the Service: Running address checks, storing your projects and polygons, and displaying your check history.
  • Account management: Verifying your email address, authenticating you on login, and processing password resets.
  • Transactional emails: Sending account verification and password-reset emails. We do not send marketing emails.
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access.
  • Service improvement: Understanding how the Service is used in aggregate (not individually) to guide product decisions.
  • Legal compliance: Complying with applicable laws, regulations, and lawful requests.

We will not use your data for any purpose not described here without first obtaining your consent.

4. Third-Party Services

RightG relies on the following third-party services. When you use the Service, data may be transmitted to these providers:

Geocoding Providers

When you submit an address for a coverage check, that address is sent to a geocoding provider to obtain geographic coordinates:

  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim — used by default. Street addresses are sent to Nominatim's public API. Nominatim is operated by the OpenStreetMap Foundation. See their privacy policy.
  • Mapbox — optionally configured by service operators. If enabled, addresses are sent to Mapbox. See Mapbox's privacy policy.

We transmit only the address string — no account information is sent to geocoding providers.

Infrastructure

  • Railway — our cloud hosting provider. Server infrastructure, database, and file storage are hosted on Railway. See Railway's privacy policy.
  • Resend — used to send transactional emails. Your email address is transmitted to Resend when we send you a verification or password-reset email. See Resend's privacy policy.

We select third-party providers that maintain appropriate data protection standards. We do not authorize these providers to use your data for their own purposes.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing or any other commercial purpose.

We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers: With third-party vendors that process data on our behalf as described in Section 4, under appropriate data processing agreements.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, court order, or lawful government request, we may disclose information necessary to comply.
  • Protection of rights: Where we believe disclosure is necessary to protect the rights, property, or safety of RightG, our users, or the public.
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of substantially all assets, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity, which will be bound by this Privacy Policy.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as necessary to provide the Service. Specifically:

  • Account data (name, email, password hash) — retained until you delete your account.
  • Projects and polygon data — retained until you delete them or delete your account.
  • Address check history — the most recent 20 checks per project are stored; older checks are not retained indefinitely.
  • Email tokens (verification, password reset) — deleted once used or expired (within 24 hours of creation).
  • Server logs — retained for up to 30 days for security purposes.

When you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required for legal or audit purposes.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Passwords are stored using PBKDF2-SHA256 with a unique salt — we cannot recover your plaintext password.
  • All data transmission between your browser and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Session tokens are stored in encrypted, HTTP-only cookies.
  • Database access is restricted to application servers.

No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will notify you as required by applicable law.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data. You can delete most data directly within the Service; for account deletion contact us.
  • Restriction: Request that we restrict processing of your data in certain circumstances.
  • Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain types of processing, including processing for direct marketing (though we do not conduct direct marketing).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@rightg.com. We will respond within 30 days. We may need to verify your identity before fulfilling a request.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or UK and believe we have not handled your data appropriately, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. Cookies & Local Storage

We use a minimal set of browser storage mechanisms:

  • Session cookie: A single encrypted, HTTP-only session cookie is set when you log in to keep you authenticated. It is deleted when you log out or your session expires.
  • localStorage: We store your UI preference (grid vs. list view on the projects page) in your browser's localStorage. This contains no personal information and is never transmitted to our servers.

We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies. No cookie consent banner is required for our essential session cookie under most privacy frameworks, but we disclose it here for full transparency.

10. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this page regularly.
